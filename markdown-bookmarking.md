# Markdown-based Bookmarking & Web Clipping

> [!note]
> Short version for now -- full guide coming.

Bookmarks rot: links die, read-later apps shut down, highlights get trapped. The markdown-based fix is to save the *content*: clip pages as clean markdown into your notes, where they're searchable, annotatable and yours.

**Tools**: the Obsidian Web Clipper (official browser extension) clips pages straight to your vault as markdown; [into.md](kb/into-md.md) converts any URL; [Turndown](kb/turndown.md) is the library if you're building your own. Add your own notes to the clip -- now it's knowledge, not a link.
